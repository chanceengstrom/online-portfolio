Brave Dogs in Soviet Space

This data viz shows the altitudes and the status of the dogs after flight for each of the soviet space dog launches.
Click on a point to recieve all the information about that flight or click on the earth to return to default view.
Red circles indicate at least one canine casualty on the flight.
Height of each point from the earth shows relative altitude.

https://spin.atomicobject.com/2015/06/12/objects-around-svg-circle-d3-js/
